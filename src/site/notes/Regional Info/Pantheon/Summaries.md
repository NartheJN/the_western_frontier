# The Prime Deities

1. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Ramor\|Ramor]], God of Life and the Sun: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Ramor\|Ramor]] is said to be the very essence of warmth and vitality in the world. He was born when the first rays of the sun touched the earth, bringing life to all living beings. His eternal mission is to nurture and protect life, ensuring that it thrives under his benevolent gaze.
2. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Suvanna\|Suvanna]], Goddess of Nature and the Moon: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Suvanna\|Suvanna]] is an ancient deity who has watched over the natural world since time immemorial. She is deeply connected to the cycles of nature, with her powers waxing and waning with the phases of the moon. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Suvanna\|Suvanna]]'s gentle touch brings harmony to the wilderness, and she is revered by those who seek to preserve the balance of the natural world.
3. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Gadeon\|Gadeon]], God of War and Storms: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Gadeon\|Gadeon]] is a fierce and unyielding god, whose temper mirrors the thunderstorms that bear his mark. He is the protector of warriors, granting them strength and courage in battle. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Gadeon\|Gadeon]]'s thunderous presence on the battlefield inspires both awe and fear.
4. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Ashaadi\|Ashaadi]], God of Magic and Mysteries: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Ashaadi\|Ashaadi]] is the enigmatic keeper of arcane knowledge and the mysteries of the universe. they are said to dwell in hidden sanctuaries accessible only to the most dedicated and worthy of spellcasters. Those who seek their wisdom must prove their dedication to the study of magic and the pursuit of ancient truths.
5. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Sirta\|Sirta]], Goddess of Knowledge and Secrets: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Sirta\|Sirta]] is the guardian of knowledge, both ancient and forbidden. She is a deity of scholars, librarians, and seekers of hidden truths. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Sirta\|Sirta]]'s temples are filled with ancient tomes and guarded secrets, and those who earn her favour are granted access to her vast repository of knowledge.
6. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Lo\|Lo]], God of Music and Artisanry: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Lo\|Lo]] is the divine patron of creativity, inspiring artists and artisans across the world. His melodies are said to have shaped the very fabric of reality, and his touch can turn mundane materials into works of art. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Lo\|Lo]] encourages the pursuit of beauty and expression in all its forms.
7. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Sefra\|Sefra]], Goddess of Fate and Death: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Sefra\|Sefra]] is a mysterious and solemn goddess, presiding over the threads of fate and the passage of souls into the afterlife. She is both a harbinger of death and a guide to the beyond. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Prime Deities/Sefra\|Sefra]] ensures that every life has a purpose and a destiny, even in the face of mortality.

# The Unspoken Gods

1. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Vaqua\|Vaqua]], God of Shadows and Trickery: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Vaqua\|Vaqua]] is a master of deception and shadows, revelling in the chaos and uncertainty he sows. He is the patron of thieves, assassins, and those who thrive in the hidden corners of society. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Vaqua\|Vaqua]]'s motives are as shrouded in darkness as his realm.
2. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Haz'ka\|Haz'ka]], Goddess of Chaos and Destruction: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Haz'ka\|Haz'ka]] is a relentless force of entropy and destruction. She delights in tearing down civilizations and causing havoc. Her followers are drawn to anarchy and upheaval, and they seek to unleash her destructive power upon the world.
3. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Vorshka\|Vorshka]], Goddess of Disease and Rot: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Vorshka\|Vorshka]] embodies the decay and sickness that plagues the living world. She thrives in filth and disease, spreading pestilence wherever she goes. Her worshipers are often healers turned corrupt, using their knowledge to inflict suffering rather than relieve it.
4. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Nipheth\|Nipheth]], God of Undead and Void: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/Nipheth\|Nipheth]] is a lord of the void, drawing power from the emptiness between worlds. He commands legions of undead and seeks to plunge the world into eternal darkness. His followers are necromancers and cultists who seek to unravel the boundaries between life and death.
5. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/The Lord of Madness\|The Lord of Madness]]: Little is known about [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Unspoken Gods/The Lord of Madness\|the Lord of Madness]], except that he is the embodiment of chaos and insanity. His presence drives mortals to madness, and his motives are incomprehensible. His cultists revel in the insanity he brings, seeking to spread his unpredictable influence.

# Dragon Lords

1. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Bahamut\|Bahamut]], Lord of Justice and Nobility: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Bahamut\|Bahamut]] is a regal and honourable dragon god, upholding principles of justice and nobility. He watches over the affairs of dragons and promotes harmony among his kind. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Bahamut\|Bahamut]]'s scales shine with the luster of purity and righteousness.
2. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Nebnemenes\|Nebnemenes]], Lord of Balance and Order: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Nebnemenes\|Nebnemenes]] is a dragon god of equilibrium and order. He seeks to maintain balance in the world and among dragonkind, ensuring that neither good nor evil becomes dominant. His followers strive for neutrality and harmony.
3. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Tiamat\|Tiamat]], Lord of Greed and Vengeance: [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Tiamat\|Tiamat]] is a fearsome and power-hungry dragon god, representing the darker aspects of draconic nature. She embodies greed, envy, and a thirst for vengeance. [[Regional Info/Pantheon/The Dragonlords/Tiamat\|Tiamat]]'s multiple heads symbolize her multifaceted desires and ambitions.
